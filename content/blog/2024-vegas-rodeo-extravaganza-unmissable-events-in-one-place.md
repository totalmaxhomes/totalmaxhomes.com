Every December, Las Vegas hosts the National Finals Rodeo (NFR), the ultimate event in the world of rodeo. This is not only the top global competition for cowboys, but also an entertainment extravaganza full of passion and joy. In 2024, Las Vegas will offer a series of exciting rodeo activities that allow you to fully experience the carnival atmosphere of the West. Here are the top 10 events you can’t miss—let’s take a look!

![Image](/blog-images/img_acce9bce3916.jpg)

## NFR LIVE: Interactive Rodeo Experience

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Rhythm & Riffs, Mandalay Bay, Las Vegas Ready to immerse yourself in the world of cowboys? This event offers free NFR viewing parties, live music performances, discounted drinks, and a variety of interactive activities that will let you experience the unique charm of rodeo every night!

![Image](/blog-images/img_0af84385f491.jpg)

Virgin Hotel Rodeo Party

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Virgin Hotel, Las Vegas Strip Virgin Hotel will once again be the gathering place for rodeo fans, offering a rich variety of entertainment and country-inspired social settings for an unforgettable cowboy feast.

![Image](/blog-images/img_9d9c01feb01c.jpg)

Bull Riders World Championship

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Las Vegas Livestock Show and Rodeo Top bull riders take on fierce bulls in a jaw-dropping competition for a $100,000 cash prize, making this an unmissable moment for every rodeo fan.

![Image](/blog-images/img_6686cebbb724.jpg)

## NFR Wrangler National Finals Rodeo

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Las Vegas Convention Center As the largest and most prestigious rodeo event in the world, the Wrangler NFR will feature fierce competition from top athletes, creating an electrifying atmosphere that’s hard to beat.

![Image](/blog-images/img_ae7ea279e074.jpg)

Cowboy Christmas - Western Gift Show

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Las Vegas Convention Center This Western-themed gift show allows you to pick up unique handcrafted goods, jewelry, and art, making it the perfect place to find one-of-a-kind holiday gifts.

![Image](/blog-images/img_718fdb563a81.png)

Rodeo Vegas Party - Zouk Nightclub

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Resorts World For those looking to enjoy a post-rodeo party, Zouk Nightclub is the place to be. With country music, live entertainment, and dazzling performances, you’ll be able to revel in the heat of the night.

![Image](/blog-images/img_c9e44c1b43d9.jpg)

Gilley’s NFR Party

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Treasure Island Hotel At Gilley’s Saloon, you can watch live NFR broadcasts with fellow cowboy enthusiasts, enjoy explosive music performances after 9 PM, and experience the best party atmosphere of NFR.

![Image](/blog-images/img_b9c93fe5fc21.jpg)

Palms Casino Resort NFR 活动

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Palms Casino Resort Palms Casino Resort will be a hot spot during NFR, offering the perfect blend of party and rodeo experiences.

![Image](/blog-images/img_b020ff1ddc59.jpg)

Tower Pink Party - The STRAT

Date: December 9, 2024 Location: The STRAT At the top of the Las Vegas Tower, join an entertaining and charitable NFR party that combines delicious food, spectacular views, and a special charitable experience.

![Image](/blog-images/img_200d76d3df6f.jpg)

Cowboyville - Mandalay Bay

Date: December 5 - December 14, 2024 Location: Mandalay Bay “Cowboyville” transports you into a fully immersive cowboy world with lively country music and dazzling performances, offering the most authentic cowboy experience.

![Image](/blog-images/img_bb1decb486ea.jpg)

![Image](/blog-images/img_c40691c43bb1.jpg)

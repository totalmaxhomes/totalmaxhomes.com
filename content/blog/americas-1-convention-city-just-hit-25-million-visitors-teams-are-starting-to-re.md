01｜250 Million Visitors in the First Four Months

In the first four months of 2026, Las Vegas welcomed more than 2.5 million convention and exhibition visitors , a 10.1% increase compared with the same period last year. .

According to the latest analysis by U.S. News & World Report , Las Vegas has surpassed Chicago and Orlando to become the No.1 convention city in the United States . In 2025 alone, the city hosted approximately 6 million convention and exhibition attendees .

Behind these numbers is more than a decade of major investment in convention infrastructure:

A $600 million upgrade of the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC), expanding exhibition space from 1.9 million to 2.5 million square feet

A new 100,000-square-foot Grand Lobby featuring digital wayfinding systems and large-scale LED displays

Climate-controlled indoor corridors connecting the North and South Halls, allowing attendees to move between exhibition areas without facing the desert heat

The Vegas Loop underground transportation system developed by The Boring Company (Elon Musk), reducing a 25-minute walk to just 2 minutes

![Image](/americas-1-convention-city-just-hit-25-million-visitors-teams-are-starting-to-re-1.png)

The airport has also been upgraded in parallel. Harry Reid International Airport now offers direct flights to more than 150 destinations worldwide . Major global exhibitions hosted annually in Las Vegas include CES, SEMA, CONEXPO-CON/AGG, World of Concrete, SHOT Show, G2E, NAB Show, and Adobe Summit.

Las Vegas has more than 150,000 hotel rooms , meaning accommodation capacity is rarely a constraint. However, “capacity” does not necessarily mean “comfort”—especially for 20–30 person business teams attending exhibitions together .

![Image](/americas-1-convention-city-just-hit-25-million-visitors-teams-are-starting-to-re-2.png)

Tony Yousfi, Chief Sales Officer of The Venetian Resort, noted that the property has served the convention market for 26 years and has invested a total of $1.5 billion in renovating its suites and convention facilities.

![Image](/americas-1-convention-city-just-hit-25-million-visitors-teams-are-starting-to-re-3.jpg)

These hotels are unquestionably world-class. However, one structural challenge remains: they are all concentrated along the Las Vegas Strip.

During major exhibitions, room availability tightens, rates climb rapidly, and teams are often split across multiple floors or even different properties. For procurement teams hosting clients and coordinating meetings throughout the exhibition, this is not always the most efficient solution.

Official data also shows an interesting trend: hosting conferences in Las Vegas increases attendance by approximately 10% on average . This means event attendance continues to grow, while hotel capacity and the overall guest experience on the Strip have not kept pace.

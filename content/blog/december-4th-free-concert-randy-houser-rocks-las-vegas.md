## A Free Concert to Ignite Your Passion

On December 4th, from 4:30 PM to 11:00 PM, Las Vegas' Fremont Street Experience will host the annual Downtown Hoedown — a free country music concert! If you’re a fan of rodeo sports, this is the grand opening you don’t want to miss. The concert kicks off the Wrangler National Finals Rodeo, launching 12 days of country music celebration. It's a paradise for both music and rodeo enthusiasts.

![Image](/blog-images/img_92e350753d31.jpg)

![Image](/blog-images/img_1fafde90c3b8.png)

Star-Studded Lineup

The December 4th lineup is nothing short of spectacular: at 5:15 PM, Chase Matthew and Jenna Paulette will kick off the show on the Main Street stage. Then, Jackson Dean will perform at the First Street stage, and at 9:15 PM, country superstar Randy Houser will take the stage with Annie Bosko to close the night with a bang! Best of all, the event is completely free and open to everyone, so you can enjoy this stunning music feast without a care in the world.

## D&D, Dreams & Desires Mansion

Looking to unwind in a dreamlike environment? D&D Dreams & Desires Mansion will surely amaze you. A perfect blend of Venetian charm and modern amenities, the mansion features luxurious attractions such as a pool bar, water benches, and a Palapa gazebo. With over 2,800 square meters of spacious space, 12 bedrooms, and 13 bathrooms, it’s an ideal venue for high-end events. It has also been selected by numerous TV shows and sports teams as a filming location, offering a taste of luxury and personalized services, making it your ultimate vacation choice.

If you love sports and entertainment, R&R Retreat and Revelation Mansion will offer you a one-stop luxury resort experience.

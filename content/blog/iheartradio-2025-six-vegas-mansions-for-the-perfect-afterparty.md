01 Las Vegas Nights: Beyond the Neon

September in Las Vegas always carries an irresistible allure.

By day, this desert city becomes a stage for conferences, exhibitions, deals, and collaborations; by night, it transforms into a neon-lit city that never sleeps, where your heartbeat syncs with the music.

This September, the iHeartRadio Music Festival will once again set the desert ablaze, immersing every music lover in melodies they’ll never forget.

A Pilgrimage for Music Lovers Worldwide

03 After the Party, You Need a Real “Home”

The music is thunderous, the crowd electric—an unforgettable night, but one that leaves you exhilarated and exhausted.

As you step out of the arena, the melodies still linger in your ears, but your body craves for a peaceful and comfortable retreat.

Just a short drive away—about 10–15 minutes—you’ll find the six private TotalMax Mansions, a “personal vacation kingdom” in Las Vegas that adds a touch of luxury and serenity to your music festival nights.

![Image](/blog-images/img_887d9d2d3df0.jpg)

🏛️ D&D, Dreams & Desires Mansion

Blending Roman charm with classic Italian elegance, D&D features a leisure pool, koi pond, Zen garden, wine cellar, gym, game room, and meeting rooms. Covering 2,833 m² with 12 bedrooms and 13 bathrooms, it can host up to 39 guests. The outdoor garden is adorned with Taihu stones, four arched bridges, streams, and bamboo groves, creating an oriental watercolor scene—perfect for weddings, parties, and corporate events.

![Image](/blog-images/img_00ec6576bf7b.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_e48f050b7479.png)

🏀 R&R, Retreat and Revelation Mansion

A haven for sports lovers, this modern minimalist mansion boasts a leisure pool, jacuzzi, waterfall slide, water wall, KTV room, full-size NCAA basketball court, terrace, and swim-up bar. Fully equipped indoors and outdoors, it can accommodates up to 48 guests—ideal for family gatherings, large team-building events, and exhibitions.

![Image](/blog-images/img_569886e4c135.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_1a55a4ceca7f.png)

🍷 O&O, Oasis Oakey Mansion

Rich in Italian architectural flair, O&O offers a waterfall pool, water slide, jacuzzi, central bar, KTV room, outdoor kitchen, and BBQ area. With 9 bedrooms and 8 bathrooms, it hosts up to 34 guests and is a sought-after filming and photography location.

![Image](/blog-images/img_832f18babe73.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_293c6c536e9a.png)

🎶 C&C, Celestial Club Mansion

Dubbed a “world-class private nightclub” by fans, C&C comes with twin waterfall pools, a poolside bar, game room, disco hall, KTV, backyard lounge, and high-end indoor/outdoor kitchens—perfect for both total relaxation and all-night parties.

![Image](/blog-images/img_ea4e8d12e47c.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_a6935d1ce4fb.png)

🪷 E&E, Enchanted Elegant Mansion

A unique blend of Jiangnan garden aesthetics and modern luxury, E&E is one of Las Vegas’ rare Chinese-style garden mansions. It features a lazy river, twin pools, lakeside lounge, meeting space, and BBQ area. With 7 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms, it’s the ultimate East-meets-West vacation retreat.

![Image](/blog-images/img_d12c48e43fd5.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_153136c49940.png)

🏯 U&U, Ultimate Utopia Mansion

The first thing you’ll see is a grand mural of Along the River During the Qingming Festival . U&U offers a cave-style jacuzzi, slide pool, KTV, and automated retractable gates. It marries traditional Chinese artistry with modern amenities, accommodating up to 34 guests.

![Image](/blog-images/img_2d21de27a127.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_0e95a29ca6b0.png)

04 Why a Mansion Is the Perfect Post-Festival Choice

Keep the vibes alive: Don't let the energy fade when the concert ends. Gather your crew: Book the entire place and party till sunrise. Freedom of space: Chill with vinyl records or throw a pool bash—the choice is yours. Prime location: Only about 4–6 miles from T-Mobile Arena, with easy access.

This September, Las Vegas isn’t just the stage for music—it’s the stage for life.

Let the iHeartRadio Music Festival ignite your night, and let the six private TotalMax Mansions give your journey a truly unique and unforgettable finale.

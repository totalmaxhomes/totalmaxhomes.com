Las Vegas — a desert city never stops buzzing with energy. By day, it’s all about the splash of pool parties; by night, it’s the frenzy of stages and lights. Here, “ordinary” has no place.

![Image](/blog-images/img_26f91681159f.jpg)

This September, pop legend Janet Jackson will light up Las Vegas with her spectacular “Together Again” world tour . She will ignite the night at the Resorts World Theatre , where her iconic voice and electrifying dance moves will instantly send the crowd into a frenzy. From Together Again to Any Time, Any Place , every track will deliver a perfect blend of nostalgia and pure power.

![Image](/blog-images/img_2be496afc068.png)

Concert Tips

Time & Venue

September 10 (Wed) and September 13 (Sat) , 8:30 PM

Resorts World Theatre , located on the north end of the Las Vegas Strip

Ticket Information

Starting at $74 (including tax). Popular dates are selling fast — book early for peace of mind.

Seat Selection

For an up-close experience, choose mid-to-front sections.

For the best view of the full light show, mid-to-rear sections are ideal.

Transportation

Convenient public and ride-share access; self-parking is also available at the theatre.

Arrive Early

The theatre’s bars and snacks are worth a visit. Arriving early lets you soak in the atmosphere.

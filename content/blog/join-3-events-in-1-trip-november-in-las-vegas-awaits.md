## DevLearn Conference & Expo

Date: November 6-8, 2024

Location ：MGM Grand Hotel and Casino, 3799 Las Vegas Blvd S.

DevLearn is the annual premier event in the field of learning and development, attracting educational technology experts from around the world this year to explore the future possibilities of learning. The event offers a variety of highlights, including campus-themed venue decor, tech showcases from 175 cutting-edge companies, a relaxing Zen zone, and an exclusive Club DL lounge to meet your relaxation needs. Interactive labs allow attendees to experience the latest technologies firsthand, inspiring innovative thinking. The 20th anniversary celebration will feature special guests, including Phil Rosenthal, creator of the food show Somebody Feed Phil , and celebrity chef Jeff Henderson, whose unique perspectives and creative insights will add flair to the event. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a newcomer in education, this event promises to bring endless inspiration.

![Image](/blog-images/img_b707c0a249b4.png)

## Construction Financial Management Conference

Date: November 6-8, 2024

Location ：Park MGM, 3370 S Las Vegas Blvd

This event is carefully curated for professionals in construction finance, co-hosted by the Associated General Contractors of America (AGC) and the Construction Financial Management Association (CFMA). The conference covers diverse topics, from taxation and accounting to cybersecurity, encompassing every aspect of the industry. Expert speakers will not only present the latest financial management strategies but will also delve into the forefront of the construction industry. Participants can earn up to 23 CPE credits, gaining both valuable knowledge and expanding their professional networks. Whether you're a veteran in construction finance or new to the field, you'll gain invaluable industry insights and connections.

![Image](/blog-images/img_7b46ef5083b4.jpg)

Summerlin Sounds Concert

Date: November 6, 6:00 pm - 9:00 pm

Location ：The Lawn at Downtown Summerlin, 1980 Festival Plaza Dr.

After a day of conferences, why not head to Downtown Summerlin for a relaxing outdoor concert? The Summerlin Sounds concert series takes place every Wednesday evening, offering lively live music and family-friendly activities. You can lie on the lawn, enjoy the soothing melodies, and unwind with family and friends. The event also features the Horse Trailer Hideout bar, serving custom cocktails to enhance your evening experience. The November 6 performance will add a touch of relaxation and joy to your trip.

Las Vegas, the “Entertainment Capital of the World,” never fails to surprise you when you least expect it:

By day, you can wander the Strip, passing by the Eiffel Tower one moment, and drifting through Venetian canals the next.

By night, when the lights come alive, stages and wine glasses make time slip away.

![Image](/blog-images/img_3bee2bf7efd8.jpg)

The allure of Las Vegas lies not only in its dazzling lights and shows, but also in its ability to embrace every dream. It can be a place of indulgent nightlife, or a serene retreat. This October, the city will once again ignite with nostalgia and passion—

When We Were Young Festival

![Image](/blog-images/img_1a4a5f144635.jpg)

When We Were Young Festival: A Return to Youth

On October 18 and 19, 2025 , the Las Vegas Festival Grounds will once again become the gathering place of rock and youth.

From its inception, this festival has been celebrated as a “live version of the 90s youth playlist.” This year’s lineup is as powerful as ever: Blink-182, Panic! At The Disco, Weezer and more will take the stage, bringing back the songs that once played on repeat in our CD players late into the night.

![Image](/blog-images/img_5a4e3001ac58.png)

When night falls, neon flickers, and tens of thousands raise their voice together in the desert city, you’ll realize that youth never truly left—it was simply waiting for the right moment to blaze again.

The festival’s passion and wildness perfectly echo the spirit of Las Vegas: bold, yet with room to breathe. After the frenzy of the day, you’ll crave a place to slow down, to rest, and to prepare for the next chapter. That’s where life and stage meet seamlessly.

![Image](/blog-images/img_cfe03057e7b7.jpg)

The Six Mansions: Exclusive Private Time Beyond the Festival

## six TotalMax Mansions

Each mansion feels like a lifestyle guide of its own—carrying the energy of the festival, while offering high-quality, private experiences.

![Image](/blog-images/img_f7f1795c0927.png)

Dreams & Desires Mansion (D&D): A Stage of Cultural Fusion

Blending Eastern and Western design, this mansion is the most inclusive space for group gatherings. The front lawn and fountain are perfect for setting up a small stage, while the backyard’s Chinese garden—adorned with Taihu rocks flown in from Suzhou—sets a tranquil vibe. The pool and pavilion often become the backdrop for impromptu jam sessions: someone strums a guitar, while friends enjoy custom barbecue prepared by the private chef and sing along to the very songs they just heard live.

![Image](/blog-images/img_00ec6576bf7b.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_3ccd9ba7edd5.jpg)

![Image](/blog-images/img_05391363a195.png)

Retreat & Revelation Mansion (R&R): The Exclusive Arena of Team Energy

This mansion’s basketball court, professional-grade soundproof KTV rooms, and large swimming pool make it an ideal spot for team gatherings after the festival. When the main stage goes quiet, the yard transforms into a second venue: some keep their energy alive on the full-sized court built by an NCAA supplier, while others belt out the night’s hits in the KTV room.

![Image](/blog-images/img_b457ef338cbd.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_b0bac492c629.png)

Oasis Oakey Mansion (O&O): A Grand Stage for Social Parties

With its soaring 30-foot living room and Italian-inspired layout, this mansion carries an undeniable sense of grandeur. In the backyard, the poolside bar and hot tub become the natural hub of evening social life. Here, guests often pop chilled bottles of Dom Pérignon, replaying the songs they just heard and turing the festival’s energy into lasting memories.

Celestial Club Mansion (C&C): The Ultimate After-Party Stage

This mansion feels tailor-made for post-festival parties: double pools with slides, an underwater bar, a professional disco hall, and even a private cinema. When the official performance ends, the real celebration begins. With just a glance, your private butler can cue the DJ booth, light up the dance floor, and unleash surround sound—creating an exclusive after-party for you and your closest friends.

![Image](/blog-images/img_71ada3e8308f.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_aba1f158f601.png)

Enchanted Elegant Mansion (E&E): A Quiet Corner for Musical Reflection

This mansion is the ideal retreat after a day of performances. Artificial hills and streams, pavilions, and riverside lounge chairs allow the city's noise to slowly fade away. Some guests play softly on a guitar in the pavilion, others savor a Napa Valley vintage recommended by the sommelier, or enjoy private yoga or meditation at dawn and dusk.

Round-the-clock butler service ensures that tea or light snacks are always at hand, making every quiet moment fully infulgent.

Ultimate Utopia Mansion (U&U): A Gentle Backstage for Family and Friends

With its open lawns and homely atmosphere, this mansion becomes an extension of “home” during the festival. Families take children to performances by day, then gather around the pool at night to light a bonfire and share favorite tracks. Children play freely on the lawn, adults toast and chat, while the kitchen thoughtfully serves warm digestive drinks and light bites. Here, festival memories are gently absorbed in warmth and laughter.

The festival takes you back to youth; the mansions let that feeling to linger.

Stage and life complement each other:

The festival satisfies your restless spirit.

The mansion restores your body and soul.

Just like Las Vegas itself, a city where dazzling neon coexists with peaceful moments that belong only to you.

When We Were Young Festival reignites the flames of youth, while the six TotalMax Mansions let that flame continue in your daily life.

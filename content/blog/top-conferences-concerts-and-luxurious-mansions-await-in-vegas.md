## NAMB National

⏰ Date: October 18-21, 2024

🏨 Venue: Caesars Palace

This is the largest annual gathering for mortgage professionals, bringing together top industry experts. You can attend workshops hosted by leading companies, hear keynotes from industry leaders, and don't miss the 50th-anniversary retro-themed closing party. It's an event mortgage professionals simply can't afford to miss!

![Image](/blog-images/img_a2831cda978f.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_35a49cf6b80e.png)

★Highlights:

NAMB Welcome Dinner

Workshops hosted by industry-leading companies

Retro 1973-themed closing party with live band performance and buffet dinner

![Image](/blog-images/img_9e874c73803b.jpg)

NCCHC

## National Correctional Health Conference

⏰ Date: October 19-24, 2024

🏨 Venue: Paris Las Vegas

The largest correctional health conference in North America will be held at Paris Las Vegas, bringing together healthcare experts from prisons, jails, and juvenile facilities. The event offers a wealth of educational sessions and exhibition areas to broaden your professional horizon through networking and learning.

![Image](/blog-images/img_ce052b7269b8.jpg)

![Image](/blog-images/img_d9197c97dcf4.png)

★Highlights:

Over 100 educational sessions

Rich networking opportunities at the exhibition area

CCHP exam with up to 32 hours of CE credits

## Brad Paisley Concert

⏰ Date: October 18-19, 2024

🏨 Venue: Encore Theater

Country music superstar Brad Paisley will perform at the Encore Theater! Known for his soulful voice and unparalleled guitar skills, this concert is sure to leave you spellbound. From hits like "Whiskey Lullaby" to "Ticks," this night will be an unforgettable experience.

![Image](/blog-images/img_89ac008a9ae2.png)

![Image](/blog-images/img_e04ecc856eb0.png)

## Lenny Kravitz

⏰ Date: October 18-26, 2024

🏨 Venue: Park Theater at Park MGM

Get ready for a night of rock and roll madness! Lenny Kravitz's residency show "Blue Electric Light" will electrify the Las Vegas sky. Experience a blend of lasers, explosions, and musical effects as you immerse yourself in his iconic hits. It’s an unforgettable night for all rock fans.

![Image](/blog-images/img_3c0ed6f1c1f0.jpg)

![Image](/blog-images/img_e04ecc856eb0.png)

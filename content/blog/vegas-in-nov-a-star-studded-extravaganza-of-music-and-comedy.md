From November 13th to 16th, Las Vegas transforms into a paradise for fans of music, comedy, and dazzling performances. With a lineup of world-class entertainers, this iconic city will be the backdrop for unforgettable nights of melody, laughter, and immersive artistic experiences. Whether you crave the rhythm of rock, the emotion of R&B, or the thrill of live comedy, you’ll find it all here.

1.The B-52s Party Extravaganza (November 13-16)

Welcome to the retro party of the year with The B-52s ! At The Venetian Theatre, prepare to dive into the upbeat sounds of “Rock Lobster” and other hits as the band brings the energy and vibe of the ’80s back to life. With their infectious beats and humorous lyrics, the B-52s will transport the crowd to a night of pure, nostalgic fun.

![Image](/blog-images/img_faef9f3d0d75.jpg)

2. Lionel Richie’s Night of Romance (November 13-16)

The legendary Lionel Richie will grace the stage at the Wynn Hotel, delivering a heartfelt journey through love and emotion. From the soulful “Endless Love” to the touching “Say You, Say Me,” Richie’s repertoire is sure to stir up warm memories and create new ones, making this an ideal evening for couples and fans of soulful ballads.

![Image](/blog-images/img_fa2e06d6bdc5.jpg)

3. Barry Manilow’s Return to Classics (November 14-16)

Pop icon Barry Manilow will perform at the Westgate Hotel, bringing timeless hits like “Copacabana” to life on stage. This concert will be an ode to the golden era of pop music, inviting the audience to sing along and relive the magic of Manilow’s most beloved songs.

![Image](/blog-images/img_e683555a8226.jpg)

4. Iliza Shlesinger’s Comedy Delight (November 15)

Comedy queen Iliza Shlesinger will captivate audiences with her witty humor and clever social observations. Her sharp insights into everyday life and relationships promise an evening of non-stop laughter – perfect for fans of stand-up comedy.

![Image](/blog-images/img_e78dfa4313c0.jpg)

5. Bob the Drag Queen’s Dazzling Show (November 15)

Prepare for a colorful and unforgettable experience with Bob the Drag Queen ! Known for blending humor, boldness, and stunning costumes, Bob’s performance offers a visually and emotionally enriching experience, making it a must-see for fans of drag shows and the LGBTQ+ community.

![Image](/blog-images/img_84ef7a0770ed.png)

6. Rex Orange County’s Heartfelt Tunes (November 15)

At The Theater at Virgin Hotels, UK singer-songwriter Rex Orange County will deliver a show filled with sincere and comforting melodies. His intimate style and heartfelt lyrics resonate deeply, offering a warm night of music that appeals especially to younger audiences.

![Image](/blog-images/img_d07248f94b54.jpg)

7. Aldo Nova’s Rock Revival (November 15)

Rock fans are in for a treat with Aldo Nova , who will bring his classic hits from the ’80s back to life. With his powerful riffs and nostalgic beats, this concert will transport attendees back to rock’s heyday, celebrating a genre that defined a generation.

![Image](/blog-images/img_5e587221bb0d.jpg)

8. Usher’s R&B Spectacle (November 15-16)

R&B sensation Usher will light up T-Mobile Arena with his electrifying stage presence and soulful performances. Songs like “U Got It Bad” are expected to resonate deeply with fans, while his seamless moves and charisma will ensure an unforgettable night of entertainment.

![Image](/blog-images/img_34291fd06b1a.jpg)

9. George Lopez’s Night of Laughter (November 15-16)

George Lopez , the beloved comedian, will bring his signature humor to The Mirage. With sharp, witty observations and cultural anecdotes, Lopez’s comedy is both relatable and hilarious, making this show a must for fans of Latino culture and comedy alike.

![Image](/blog-images/img_d33f85212392.jpg)

10. Adele’s Soulful Serenade (November 15-23)

In a highly anticipated residency at Caesars Palace, Adele will deliver an emotionally charged performance, gracing the audience with hits like “Someone Like You.” Her voice, rich with emotion and power, will fill the venue and captivate fans in a concert experience that is bound to be both intimate and grand.

![Image](/blog-images/img_81a03ff8a33f.png)

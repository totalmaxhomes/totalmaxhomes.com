Las Vegas, a city people always want to return to.

Some come to Las Vegas for its dazzling nightlife. thers fly in for a performance or a Michelin-starred dinner. But for professionals in the beauty industry, every July brings another reason to visit: Cosmoprof North America .

![Image](/vegas-is-not-just-for-the-night-cosmoprof-and-the-citys-global-business-arena-1.png)

Cosmoprof North America Las Vegas 2026, the annual gathering of the beauty industry in the Americas

From July 13 to 15, the 23rd Cosmoprof North America will take place at the Mandalay Bay Convention Center. As the largest and most influential B2B beauty trade show in the Americas, it covers skincare, cosmetics, haircare, fragrance, nail products, organic beauty, and more. From established brands to upstream manufacturers and suppliers, the entire beauty supply chain comes together under one roof.

The exhibition is divided into two main sections: Cosmoprof North America , focusing on finished products and brands, and Cosmopack North America , dedicated to raw materials, packaging, OEM/ODM manufacturing, and production equipment. Whether you are a brand searching for your next breakthrough formula or a supplier looking to connect with North American buyers, three days can open the door to valuable partnerships.

This year's highlights are especially noteworthy. The K-Beauty zone continues to expand. For the first time, the CosmoTalks forum will be held directly on the exhibition floor, bringing expert insights and product discovery together in one space. The Buyer Meetings program has also expanded to Las Vegas, offering both pre-scheduled and on-site meetings to improve networking efficiency. More than 1,000 exhibitors and over 26,000 professional attendees from more than 100 countries are expected.

In simple terms, if you're in the beauty industry and you're not here during these three days, there's a good chance your competitors will be.

![Image](/vegas-is-not-just-for-the-night-cosmoprof-and-the-citys-global-business-arena-2.png)

But the exhibition is only part of the story. Where you stay at night shapes the quality of the entire experience.

Inside the halls of Mandalay Bay, the lights are bright, the crowds are constant, and the pace rarely slows. After a full day, both your mind and body need a chance to recharge. What you need is more than a standard hotel room. You need a space with a pool, a kitchen, and a living room. A place where your team can review the day together, or simply relax in peace.

Las Hawaii Mansions, a collection of seven private mansions, offers exactly that.

![Image](/vegas-is-not-just-for-the-night-cosmoprof-and-the-citys-global-business-arena-3.png)

C&C Celestial Club Mansion | The “charging station” for exhibition teams

Twin pools, double waterfalls, a waterslide, and a private disco room create the perfect setting to unwind after a full day on the exhibition floor. With nine bedrooms and an industrial-grade kitchen, the mansion can comfortably host team dinners and private gatherings. An Uber ride from the Strip costs only about ten dollars, making C&C Celestial Club Mansion a practical choice that balances convenience, comfort, and value.

D&D Dreams & Desires Mansion | The ideal venue for brand events

Venetian-style architecture, Italian classical design, and imported Taihu stones with four traditional stone bridges from Suzhou come together in a unique blend of Eastern and Western aesthetics. The mansion features a world-class resort-style pool, a wine cellar with capacity for up to 3,000 bottles, and a south lawn that can accommodate events for up to 500 guests. If you're planning a brand launch, client reception, or appreciation event during the exhibition, D&D Dreams & Desires Mansion provides a setting that rises to the occasion.

E&E Enchanted Elegant Mansion | Jiangnan water town in the desert

The only Chinese classical garden-style mansion in Las Vegas. White walls, gray tiles, bamboo beams, and flowing waterfalls create a tranquil setting, while the main hall showcases Southern China, a masterpiece by Wu Guanzhong. Two connected resort-style pools are linked by a rock cave, and at night, LED lighting transforms the lazy river into a galaxy-like scene. With seven bedrooms, a formal dining room that seats 25 guests, and more than 70 security cameras operating around the clock, safety is never compromised.

O&O Oasis Oakey Mansion | Italian romance through a Hollywood lens

With over 30-foot-high ceilings, stained-glass arched windows, elegant colonnades, and long corridors, stepping into O&O Oasis Oakey Mansion feels like walking into a classic romantic film. The resort-style water features are equally impressive, including waterfalls, rock caves, a swim-up bar, a large jacuzzi, and Hawaiian-style palapa shelters. The mansion has also served as a filming location for multiple productions, making it easy to capture cinematic moments throughout your Las Vegas stay.

U&U Ultimate Utopia Mansion | A thousand-year scroll of Along the River During the Qingming Festival

Upon arrival, you are welcomed by an eight-foot-high, 100-foot-long mural of Along the River During the Qingming Festival . The theme is unmistakable: recreating the elegance of a thousand years of Chinese cultural heritage. Cave-style spa pools and illuminated water features create a refreshing retreat from the desert heat. With eight bedrooms, a resort-style pool, and a private karaoke room, the mansion is equally well suited for group trips and family gatherings.

Recently opened for occupancy, Y&Y Mansion is more than a residential property. It also serves as the future global headquarters base of TotalMax Homes LLC. T&T Mansion | Inspired by the Summer Palace, with imperial elegance
